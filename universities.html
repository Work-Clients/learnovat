How It Works

STEP 1

Students Check Their Eligibility

Students complete a short survey and
get matched to programs and schools.

STEP 2

Students Apply to Schools

Students select a school and program,
complete their profile, pay their fees,
and submit the proper documentation.

STEP 3

Students Get Accepted

The application is reviewed by the
school and a letter of acceptance is
issued.

STEP 4

Student Applies for Visa

Learnovate experts guide the student
through the visa application process.

STEP 5

Student Journey Begins

The student sets off with bags packed
ready to start their adventure on your
campus.
